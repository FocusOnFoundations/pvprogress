Section 65583

Government Code Provision

Housing Element

Compliance

The housing element shall consist of an identification and analysis of existing and projected

housing needs and a statement of goals, policies, quantified objectives, financial resources, and

scheduled programs for the preservation, improvement, and development of housing.

Sec. 7: Goals,

Policies, Programs

The housing element shall identify adequate sites for housing, including rental housing, factory-

built housing, mobilehomes, and emergency shelters, and shall make adequate provision for the

existing and projected needs of all economic segments of the community.
